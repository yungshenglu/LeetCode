# LC-204 - Count Primes

Count the number of prime numbers less than a *non-negative* number, `n`.

> * Difficulty: **EASY**

---
## Examples

```
Input: 10
Output: 4
Explanation:
There are 4 prime numbers less than 10, they are 2, 3, 5, 7.
```

---
## Solutions

1. Naive Approach
    * Time complexity: $O(\sqrt{n})$
    * Space complexity: $O(1)$
